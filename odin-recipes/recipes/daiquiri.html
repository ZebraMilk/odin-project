The Daiquiri

Story

The daiquiri was made first in Cuba, but the concept of a crispy cold combination of rum-sugar-lime is as old as rum itself. One of the most delightful drinks, really lets the rum sing through, whether you use a clean, white rum or something funkier.

Ingredients

  • 6 cL white rum, Cuban is the most classic choiuce here (Havana Club, Bacardi)
  • 2 cL 1:1 simple syrup
  • 3 cL lime juice
  • a pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Chill your favorite cocktail glass with ice and water.
  2. In a shaker tin, combine the rum, lime, and sugar,
  3. Dramatically sprinkle your pinch of salt into the shaker.
  4. Add a dutiful amounbt of ice.
  5. Shake the ever-loving shite out of your daiquiri, 15-20 seconds.
  6. Drain your chilling glass and strain your Daiquiri.
  7. Drink it while it is still laughing at you!
